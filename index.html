Your Journey Starts Here

Joining the Voice For Peace Community is a straightforward process. Here's how to get started on your path to service.

1

Explore Opportunities

Browse open volunteer assignments by country, sector, and required skills to find your best match.

2

Submit Your Application

Complete your online application including personal statements and a resume.

3

Interview & Nomination

An interview with a recruiter and nomination to a specific program.

4

Training & Departure
